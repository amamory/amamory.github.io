Research Topics

VLSI Design and Testing

My research interests include design for test and fault tolerance for VLSI designs, including MultiProcessor System-on-Chip (MPSoCs) and Network-on-Chip (NoC).

Robotics

My research interests include middleware and low level software for robotics applications, reliable robotics applications, robotics for humanitarian applications and environmental monitoring.
