Robotics

My research interests include middleware and low level software for robotics applications, reliable robotics applications, robotics for humanitarian applications and environmental monitoring. This research is developed in cooperation with Laboratório de Sistema Autônomos - LSA.

Here is a lists os research topics and current design projects I am involved.

Research Projetcs

  • Donnie: robot programming environment for visually impared people;
  • Picasso: manipulador robótico para processo de pintura aplicado ao setor naval e petróleo;
  • Autonomous landmine detection robot;
  • Integração de veículos autônomos e redes de sensores para aquisição de dados remotos para prevenção e mitigação de desastres naturais;
    • Autonomous boat for environmental monitoring and disaster mitigation;
    • Flood forecast system for urban scenarios based on social data;
