VLSI Design and Testing

My research interests include design for test and fault tolerance for VLSI designs, including MultiProcessor System-on-Chip (MPSoCs) and Network-on-Chip (NoC). This research is developed in cooperation with Grupo de Apoio ao Projeto de Hardware - GAPH.

Here is a lists os research topics and current design projects I am involved.

Research Projetcs

  • Fault tolerant MPSoC;
  • Publish/subscribe programing for MPSoCs;
  • Testable resilient architetures;
  • Control theory for self-adaptable MPSoC;
